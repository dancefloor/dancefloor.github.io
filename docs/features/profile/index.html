#Timeline

Timeline is the idea of making the dancefloor academy website more like a social media, just like twitter or facebook. The timeline is a news feed of all actions taken by the user, just like a log, example

Liked x video - Tuesday, 24th march 2020 Completed macarena challenge - Wednesday, 27th march 2020 Register for Sensual Bachata level 3 - Saturday, 24th march 2020

#Payments

The user can view a list of all his or her payments and their status (completed, pending, waiting or cancelled). In addition each payment has a link for the detail information of regarding that payment (the bill) as well as an option to download the bill in PDF format
